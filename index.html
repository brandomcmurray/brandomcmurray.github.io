About

Brandon McMurray is an early career researcher in the field of molecular genetics. He is currently employed as an Assistant Research Technologist in the Maass Lab of the Genetics and Genome Biology program at the SickKids Research Institute in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He received his Bachelor of Science in Honours Biology from the University of Waterloo in Ontario, Canada and has a background in bird ecology, plant genetics & ecology, bacterial genetics, and human genetics. Brandon is fueled by his passion for non-coding genomics. His familiarity with wet and dry lab methods makes him an especially versatile team member.
